Description

Delicious vegetarian alternative to the famous traditional Italian dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 cup besciamella sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella
  • 1/2 large yellow onion, minced
  • 2 medium carrots, sliced
  • 1 tsp. salt, + more to taste
  • 1 tsp. pepper, + more to taste
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste (55g)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 200g veggie meat
  • 1/2 tsp. dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp. dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp. dried rosemary
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • 500g lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. In a large pot, cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the olive oil in the same pot over medium heat. Add the onion and saute until translucent about 2 mins.
  3. Stir in the carrots, salt and pepper. Add the garlic and stir to combine, then add the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, broth, veggie meat, basil, oregano, rosemary and stir to combine.
  4. Bring to a boil, then cover, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 40 mins, until the veggie ragu is thickened.
  5. In a casserole add 1 layer of ragu sauce, lasagna sheet on top, again a layer of ragu, make sure you cover it well, a layer of mozzarella, and repeat this step until all the ingredients are added to the casserole. Finish off with sprinkling chopped parsley on top.
  6. Cover the casserole with aluminum foil, put it in the oven and leave it in the oven on medium heat for 40 mins.
  7. Enjoy!
